About Me

Bridging strategy, technology
and execution

I'm a Principal Architect at Cognizant's Intuitive Operations & Automation practice, with 22+ years of experience that started with Visual C++ and evolved through Oracle Siebel CRM, Salesforce, Microsoft Dynamics, and the Power Platform — always staying at the intersection of business context and emerging technology.

Having served as Developer, Technology Lead, Architect, Consultant, Scrum Master, and Technical Program Manager, I bring a ground-up and strategic view to every engagement. This breadth enables me to bridge business objectives, technology strategy, and delivery execution with clarity and precision across industries including telecom, manufacturing, retail, travel & hospitality, banking & insurance, life sciences, loyalty, and healthcare.

My leadership philosophy is "coaching people to think, not just do" — inspired by the belief that "the future is already here, it's just not evenly distributed." I translate that mindset into empowerment, helping teams build confidence, capability, and ownership.
