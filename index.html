I'm a Principal Architect at Cognizant's Intuitive Operations & Automation practice, with 22+ years of experience spanning Tech Mahindra, Infosys, and Cognizant — a journey that started with Visual C++ and evolved through Oracle Siebel CRM, Salesforce, Microsoft Dynamics, and the Power Platform, always staying at the intersection of business context and emerging technology. This breadth enables me to bridge business objectives, technology strategy, and delivery execution with clarity and precision across industries including telecom, manufacturing, retail, travel & hospitality, banking & insurance, life sciences, loyalty, and healthcare.

My leadership philosophy is "coaching people to think, not just do" — inspired by the belief that "the future is already here, it's just not evenly distributed." I translate that mindset into empowerment, helping teams build confidence, capability, and ownership.
